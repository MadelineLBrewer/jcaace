Mechanical

What We Do

We strive to be an integrated HVAC solutions partner that provides customized mechanical engineering for buildings. We are committed to being the most trusted member of your project team by offering dependable, lasting, and cost-effective mechanical solutions. It is important to us that we provide you with value through our expertise in mechanical engineering that is constructible and effective at keeping occupants happy and healthy in your building. We utilize 3D modeling and clash detecting software to ensure our designs are both innovative, functional, constructible, and coordinated with all systems in the building.

Experienced and Multi-Disciplined Team

Our Mechanical Engineering team brings experience to the table to help select and design the best HVAC system for your building, while keeping budget constructability, sustainability, and life cycle costs in mind. JCAA’s Mechanical Engineering team has decades of experience in all systems ranging from package stand alone systems to central plant 4 pipe systems. Through design JCAA understands that the best system is the one that balances the wants of the owner while not sacrificing comfort throughout to occupants.

    We offer full design services, including:

  • Design of Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning Systems
  • Central Plant Systems Design Down to Package system Designs.
  • Mechanical Load Calculations & Building Energy Modeling
  • Variable Refrigerant Flow System Design
  • ASHRAE 34 Refrigerant Density Calculations – by Space and Refrigerant classification
  • Controls Flow Diagrams & Sequence of Operations (SOO’s).
  • Commissioning of Mechanical & Lighting Systems.
  • Mechanical Specifications – Book and on the Project Drawings
  • Smoke Control & Smoke Removal Design
  • High Efficiency HVAC Design for LEED & Well
  • Geothermal HVAC systems
