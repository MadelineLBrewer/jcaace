Multi-Family

We specialize in providing engineering solutions for multi-family residential projects, including apartments, condos and townhomes. Our team has extensive experience in designing and consulting on projects that range from high-rise buildings to low-rise communities. We understand the unique challenges and codes that come with multi-family projects, and we work closely with architects and developers to ensure that our solutions are cost-effective and meet the needs of residents.
