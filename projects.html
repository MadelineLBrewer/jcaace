LEED

Our staff has designed many LEED buildings from Platinum to Certified. Each design is customized to the needs of the owner and end user. We incorporate many design elements to help a building achieve LEED status. In our electrical designs we implement daylighting, light pollution reduction and high-efficiency lighting such as lighting controls, occupancy sensors and dimming ballasts. In our mechanical and plumbing design we incorporate high-efficiency HVAC including high-efficiency furnaces and water heaters, zoning controls, water conservation and CO2 emissions reduction.
