Electrical

JCAA's Lighting Design Professionals integrate functional and aesthetic illumination into a project's overall architectural objectives. Our approach balances the architectural aesthetic aspirations and desired lighting effects with both performance and energy code compliance requirements. We strive to arrive at a mutually agreed upon design criteria to ensure to competing priorities of architectural design, lighting effect and/or issues involving energy code compliance are established early in the project. Our commitment to and experience on "pre-planning" of project interior spaces provides tremendous value to our client and the project. Furthermore, the blending of lighting and associated control systems has become a necessity from both an energy and energy code as well as a maintenance standpoint. This can include "local" as well as "system" approach to control. Often times the most economical resolution is a combination of both.

Structural

Our mission is to provide interdisciplinary coordination value to our clients by problem solving across all trades in design rather than in the field, reducing or eliminating costly change orders. We aim to save our clients’ time by reducing their need to coordinate multiple consultants as we work together in-house. Our focus is on delivering our clients quality products on time and within budget. By using cost-effective designs, we provide our clients with a project tailored to their specific needs.

Experience: With a diverse portfolio of projects and experience, we can adapt to all client needs. We are actively involved from project inception and feasibility to construction documents through construction administration, striving to provide value to our clients which include architects, developers, and contractors.
